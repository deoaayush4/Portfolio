Spotting Cafes: Your Perfect Study & Work Spot, Instantly.

Finding the ideal cafe or restaurant to study, work, or collaborate can be a challenge. 'Spotting Cafes' is an innovative app designed to seamlessly connect individuals with available, suitable spaces, benefiting both the user and the establishment.

🤔 The Problem We Solve

Students and professionals often struggle to find a conducive environment outside their home or office for focused work or group study. Issues include:

  • 📍 Not knowing which cafes have suitable seating or quiet zones.
  • 🚶‍♀️ Arriving at a cafe only to find it fully occupied.
  • 🕒 Difficulty in booking a guaranteed spot for a specific time or duration.
  • 🤷‍♀️ Cafes often have empty seats during off-peak hours but struggle to attract customers needing short-term space.

💡 Our Innovative Solution

'Spotting Cafes' provides a real-time platform where users can:

  • 👀 **View Availability:** See which cafes/restaurants near them have available seats (individual or group tables).
  • 📊 **Filter & Discover:** Filter by criteria like "quiet zone," "Wi-Fi availability," "power outlets," "group tables," or "outdoor seating."
  • 📅 **Book Slots:** Secure a specific table or spot for a desired duration.
  • ☕ **Minimum Order Requirement:** To ensure mutual benefit, users agree to a minimum order (e.g., a coffee or snack) upon booking.

This creates a win-win scenario, providing guaranteed space for customers and guaranteed revenue for businesses.

💰 Benefits for All

  • 👩‍💻 **For Users:**
    • Guaranteed study/work space.
    • Reduced time wasted searching for a spot.
    • Access to diverse working environments.
  • 🏨 **For Cafes/Restaurants:**
    • Increased foot traffic and new customer base.
    • Monetization of otherwise empty tables during off-peak hours.
    • Improved operational efficiency by managing seat occupancy.
    • Guaranteed revenue through minimum order requirements.
